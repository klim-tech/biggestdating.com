Why being nice while dating Russian women is important

The legendary football manager Leonard Donavar once coined the phrase: “Being strong is being nice”.

It may get you thinking about how accurate such an adage can be in relation to football, but it is also important when it comes to courting and to family life in general. You are bound to appear more attractive to the woman you are with if you show unselfishness and kindness to her on a daily basis.

Such attributes bear a universal attractiveness. A study was conducted by the University of Michigan on June 17, 2014, and it turned out that kindness was one of the top three qualities appreciated the most by women in marriage or in a relationship. Kindness allows building trust in a relationship, it allows the people showing kindness to be loved in return.

Being nice and kind to your Russian wife or girlfriend will not just boost your attractiveness in her eyes on the spur of a moment. It will lay a strong foundation on which your whole future together may thrive and prosper. Being nice and kind to your dear lady will also serve as a preemptive factor allowing you to eliminate causes of possible domestic conflicts. This way you will be able to avoid a dispute before it even originates. A petty annoyance will never ignite into a full-blown dispute if you show enough kindness and compassion to your Russian spouse.

It is also true that in many cases it is easier said than done. Being nice and kind very often involves a certain sacrifice that you will have to knowingly offer to the Russian bride you are dating. If you want to successfully date Russian women you better learn how to treat these Russian ladies with respect. It will require a certain altruism on your part, though at the same time you need to bear in mind that your kindness will eventually pay back to you in full, if your lady truly loves you.

Also bear in mind that there are two possible issues. First of all, some people can be so obtrusive with their nicety that it may seem to be rude. Sometimes a person just wants to be left alone to his own thoughts, including in marriage. Secondly, some people may be very insincere about their kindness and will just use it as a tool to gain certain favors from other people. If your wife is such a type, you are in a great trouble. Actually it is quite easy to check if your wife is a superficial individual with a hidden agenda. If she talks nicely to a person and then has all sorts of rude things to say about this person behind his back – she is this wicked type. You better elope with someone else before it is too late.

Another interesting phenomenon that is worth mentioning – overly rude people who actually prefer to hide that they are very kind by nature, thinking it is their detrimental and deplorable weakness. Usually such people had a trauma, a certain catharsis at an early stage of their life. Someone may have ruthlessly hurt their feelings when they were demonstrating kindness and nice attitudes.

It may have happened in the previous marriage or even at work. Such a person may have come to the conclusion that being kind to another individual, even to a spouse, is an unpardonable weakness that should be avoided at all costs. Observe how your spouse treats animals – usually such people with the “double bottom” are very kind to animals, especially to cats and dogs. If your wife is very considerate with your pet but is rather rude to you, you may need to talk to her about this possible trauma in the past. Usually it is a delicate subject, so you should approach it very cautiously. The progress and the healing will be slow, it takes time to recover.

What do you get in return?

You also have the right to expect kindness in return. Actually there are many tell-tale signs that will help you assess how kind or how nice your Russian wife really is. Usually Russian dating sites have the profile of many nice and kind women, but is your lady the type? Observe how she talks to waiters, colleagues, friends. See how often she becomes rude to total strangers. There are many clues that you can gather about your wife just by observing her regular attitude towards other people.
